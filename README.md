<h1 align="center">ShapeShift</h1>

Welcome to <b> ShapeShift </b>, your ultimate fitness companion designed to elevate your wellness journey! Whether you're a beginner or a fitness enthusiast, our app is tailored to meet your needs, providing a seamless experience on your fitness quest.

- Firebase: For backend services including authentication, database, and cloud storage.
- GitHub: For version control and project management.
